# Changelog / Release Notes

User-facing highlights from MeteoraBot's development. MeteoraBot ships frequently; this is a curated summary of notable, user-facing changes. Full write-ups (with dates) live on the blog: **[meteorabot.com/blog](https://meteorabot.com/blog)**.

> Engine-internal research posts (forecasting, edge modeling, self-improvement work) are covered on the blog but omitted here by design.

## Highlights

### Trading & modes
- **Spin & Bet** live-weather mini-games are live.
- **Three trading modes** — continuous, burst, and manual — inspired by how the best wallets operate.
- **Burst mode** — set-and-forget bounded buy-in.
- **Position memory across restarts** — the engine remembers and keeps managing its open positions after a restart.

### Reliability
- **Engine v2** — a major update to the trading engine.
- **Browser heartbeat** — keeps your session alive (and conserves resources) while the tab is open.
- **Hong Kong resolution indicator** — a live gauge for the number that settles Hong Kong markets.

### Trust & safety
- **Anti-abuse enforcement** — accounts abusing the engine may be restricted.
- **Copycat advisory** — beware of fake MeteoraBot sites; use only [meteorabot.com](https://meteorabot.com).

### Roadmap
- Ongoing: broadening the strategy library and benchmarking new approaches. See [What's on the build list](https://meteorabot.com/blog).
